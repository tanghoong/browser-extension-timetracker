How It Works

Time Tracker monitors your browsing activity and records time spent on websites you choose to track.

Time Tracking Rules

Time is counted only when ALL of these are true:

  • The tab with a tracked website is active (selected)
  • The browser window is focused
  • You are not idle (no mouse/keyboard inactivity)

Visit Counting

A visit is counted when:

  • You navigate to a tracked site from a non-tracked site
  • You return to a tracked tab after 30+ seconds of inactivity

Privacy

100% Local Storage: All your data stays on your device. Nothing is uploaded to external servers.

No Tracking: We don't collect any information about your browsing habits.

Open Source: The code is available for inspection on GitHub.

FAQs

Why is my tracked time less than expected?

The extension only counts time when the tab is active and focused. If you switch tabs, minimize the window, or become idle, tracking pauses.

How do I add a website to track?

Go to Settings → enter the domain (e.g., youtube.com) → click Add Site.

Can I track incognito/private browsing?

This depends on your browser settings. You may need to enable the extension in incognito mode.

How do I export my data?

In the Dashboard, select your time period and optional site filter, then click "Export CSV".

Does this slow down my browser?

No. The extension uses minimal resources (<1% CPU, <50MB memory).
